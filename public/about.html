WHERE SHREWSBURY GATHERS
The Boathouse is one of Shrewsbury’s most loved historic pubs. Its stunning location on the banks of the River Severn allows it magnificent views of the river and the Quarry park.
THE BOATSHED
The boatshed is the private party room for the boathouse, it is right on the water’s edge on the banks of the River Severn. Big enough for seating 28 guests inside and more out on the terrace it’s great for birthday parties, christenings, friends’ get togethers, or any excuse for a party you can think of.
The boatshed has its own buffet style menu, this changes seasonally + can be tweaked to suit the occasion.
